App to interact with DinoMail.

DinoMail is an open source mail manager that you
can get at https://github.com/nanoy42/dinomail. This
is the android app that interact with the server.
Features:
* Create, modify and delete domains. Verify DKIM DMARC and SPF
* Create, modify and delete users. Change password of users.
* Create, modify and delete aliases.
